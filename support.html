Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a congregation?

Open the app and tap "Start Congregation" on the home screen.

How do I join a nearby congregation?

You will receive automatic notifications when a congregation starts within your range.

How do I change the search radius?

Go to Settings and adjust the search radius.

Does the app require account registration?

No, the app does not require any registration.

The app is not working properly

Make sure Location Services and Notifications are enabled for the app.
